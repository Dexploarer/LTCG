## Featured Archetypes

The launch set includes five unique archetypes, each with distinct playstyles:

- **Infernal Dragons** - Aggressive fire-based monsters with powerful burn effects
- **Abyssal Depths** - Control-oriented water creatures with freezing mechanics
- **Iron Legion** - Defensive earth constructs with high durability
- **Storm Riders** - Swift wind units focused on speed and evasion
- **Necro Empire** - Dark undead army with graveyard recursion
