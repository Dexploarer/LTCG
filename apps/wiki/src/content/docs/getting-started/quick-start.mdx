## Welcome to LTCG

LTCG is a strategic trading card game where you build decks around powerful archetypes and battle opponents in real-time matches.

## Your First Deck

Every new player starts with a starter deck. You can choose from five archetypes:

| Archetype | Playstyle | Difficulty |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| Infernal Dragons | Aggressive burn | Easy |
| Abyssal Depths | Control & freeze | Medium |
| Iron Legion | Defensive walls | Easy |
| Storm Riders | Speed & evasion | Medium |
| Necro Empire | Graveyard recursion | Hard |

## Basic Gameplay

### Turn Structure

1. **Draw Phase** - Draw a card from your deck
2. **Main Phase** - Play cards, activate effects
3. **Battle Phase** - Attack with your monsters
4. **End Phase** - Resolve end-of-turn effects

### Card Types

- **Monsters** - Your main attackers and defenders
- **Spells** - One-time effects that resolve immediately
- **Traps** - Set face-down and activate on triggers
- **Field** - Continuous effects that change the battlefield

### Winning the Game

Reduce your opponent's Life Points from 8000 to 0 to win!
