Build your perfect deck with our interactive deck builder. Add cards, track your dust cost, and export deck codes to share.

## Deck Rules

- **Minimum**: 45 cards
- **Maximum**: 60 cards
- **Copy limit**: 3 copies per card (1 for Limited cards)
- **Banned cards**: Cannot be added

## How to Use

1. **Search cards** using the search bar and filters on the left
2. **Click a card** to add it to your deck
3. **Track your progress** with the card count and mana curve
4. **Export** your deck code to share or import into the game

## Deck Building Tips

- Start with a focused archetype for consistency
- Include a good level curve - don't go too heavy on high-level monsters
- Balance monsters, spells, and traps for versatility
- Check the [meta tier list](/meta/) to see what's currently strong
