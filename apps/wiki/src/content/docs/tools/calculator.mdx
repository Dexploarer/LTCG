## Crafting Tips

1. **Wait for nerfs** - Nerfed cards give full dust refunds for 2 weeks
2. **Craft playsets** - If you need a card, you probably need 3 copies
3. **Prioritize archetypes** - Focus dust on one deck at a time
4. **Avoid prismatic early** - Cosmetics come after a complete collection
