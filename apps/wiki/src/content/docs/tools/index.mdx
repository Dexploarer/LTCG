## Tips

- **Deck Builder**: Your deck must have 45-60 cards. Limited cards can only have 1 copy.
- **Card Comparison**: Best stats are highlighted in green, worst in red.
- **Calculator**: Toggle prismatic mode for 4x crafting/disenchant values.
