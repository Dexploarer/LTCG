## Launch Set Archetypes

The LTCG launch set features five unique archetypes, each with distinct playstyles and mechanics.

---

## Infernal Dragons

**Attribute:** Fire | **Playstyle:** Aggressive Burn

The Infernal Dragons archetype focuses on overwhelming aggression and direct damage. Their monsters hit hard and their spells burn through your opponent's life points quickly.

### Signature Mechanic
**Infernal Rage** - When your Infernal Dragon monsters destroy an opponent's monster, deal additional burn damage.

### Key Cards
- **Infernal Overlord Vyraxis** (Legendary) - The boss monster of the archetype
- **Apocalypse Dragon** (Legendary) - Devastating board clear
- **Volcanic Lair** (Field) - Boosts all fire monsters

---

## Abyssal Depths

**Attribute:** Water | **Playstyle:** Control

The Abyssal Depths archetype excels at controlling the battlefield through freezing effects and card advantage. They slow down aggressive decks while building up to massive late-game threats.

### Signature Mechanic
**Freeze** - Frozen monsters cannot attack or change battle position for one turn.

### Key Cards
- **Tidal Emperor Nereus** (Legendary) - Freeze all opponent monsters
- **Leviathan of the Abyss** (Legendary) - Massive defensive wall
- **Abyssal Trench** (Field) - Generate card advantage

---

## Iron Legion

**Attribute:** Earth | **Playstyle:** Defensive

The Iron Legion archetype builds impenetrable defenses while slowly grinding down opponents. Their high-DEF monsters are difficult to destroy and their support cards reward blocking attacks.

### Signature Mechanic
**Fortify** - Increase DEF of all Iron Legion monsters on the field.

### Key Cards
- **Iron Marshal Ferrum** (Legendary) - Protect all your monsters
- **Iron Emperor Maximus** (Legendary) - Highest DEF in the game
- **Iron Fortress** (Field) - Double your DEF bonuses

---

## Storm Riders

**Attribute:** Wind | **Playstyle:** Tempo

The Storm Riders archetype focuses on speed and evasion. Their monsters attack multiple times, dodge effects, and overwhelm opponents before they can set up defenses.

### Signature Mechanic
**Swift** - Can attack directly even if opponent controls monsters.

### Key Cards
- **Storm Archon Zelos** (Legendary) - Multiple attacks per turn
- **Tempest Archon** (Legendary) - Untargetable by effects
- **Storm's Eye** (Field) - All your monsters gain Swift

---

## Necro Empire

**Attribute:** Dark | **Playstyle:** Recursion

The Necro Empire archetype builds armies from the graveyard. Their monsters return from death, and their spells sacrifice weak units to summon powerful undead.

### Signature Mechanic
**Undying** - When this monster is destroyed, return it to your hand at end of turn.

### Key Cards
- **Death Lord Mortis** (Legendary) - Revive multiple monsters
- **Lich King Absolute** (Legendary) - Cannot be permanently destroyed
- **Necropolis** (Field) - All your monsters gain Undying
