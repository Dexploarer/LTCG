## Tournament Formats

### Conquest

Bring 3 decks, win with each to advance.

**Rules:**
- Submit 3 different archetype decks
- Win with all 3 to win the match
- Winning deck cannot be used again
- Opponent sees your deck choices

### Last Hero Standing

Bring 3 decks, play until eliminated.

**Rules:**
- Submit 3 different archetype decks
- Keep playing winning deck
- Switch decks after a loss
- First to lose all 3 decks loses

### Standard

Simple best-of-3 with sideboard.

**Rules:**
- Bring 1 main deck + 15-card sideboard
- Best of 3 games
- Sideboard between games

## Tournament Structure

### Swiss Rounds

Most tournaments use Swiss pairing:

- Everyone plays all rounds
- Paired against similar record
- Final standings by record + tiebreakers

### Single Elimination

Top cut after Swiss or direct bracket:

- Lose once, you're out
- Higher seed chooses play/draw
- Winner advances

### Double Elimination

Bracket with losers bracket:

- First loss sends to losers bracket
- Second loss eliminates
- Grand finals between brackets

## Qualification Events

### How to Qualify

Major tournaments require qualification:

1. **Rank requirement** - Must be Diamond+ for some events
2. **Qualifier tournaments** - Win local qualifiers
3. **Points** - Accumulate tournament points through season

### Championship Points

Earn points from all tournaments:

| Placement | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| 1st | 100 |
| 2nd | 75 |
| Top 4 | 50 |
| Top 8 | 30 |
| Top 16 | 15 |

Top point earners qualify for Seasonal Championship.

## Tournament Rules

### Deck Submission

- Decks must be submitted before tournament starts
- No changes after submission
- Deck codes locked and verified

### Time Limits

| Format | Per Game | Per Match |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| Best of 1 | 15 min | 15 min |
| Best of 3 | 12 min | 45 min |
| Conquest | 10 min | 50 min |

### Disconnect Policy

- 2-minute reconnection window
- 3 disconnects = match loss
- Judge can grant extensions

### Conduct

- No outside assistance
- No screen sharing in online events

## Tips for Tournament Success

1. **Practice sideboarding** - Critical for Bo3
2. **Know the meta** - Prepare for common decks
3. **Test conquest lineup** - Ensure deck coverage
4. **Manage time** - Don't run out in complex games
5. **Stay calm** - Tournament nerves are real
