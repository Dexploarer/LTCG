Story mode offers a single-player campaign experience with narrative content, rewards, and unique challenges.

## Overview

The story mode follows the lore of the LTCG world through interactive card battles against AI opponents.

### Features

- Narrative-driven gameplay
- Unlock exclusive cards and cosmetics
- Learn game mechanics progressively
- Play at your own pace
- No internet required (after download)

---

## Campaign Structure

### Acts and Chapters

The story is divided into **Acts**, each containing multiple **Chapters**.

| Act | Chapters | Theme | Archetypes Featured |
|-----|----------|-------|---------------------|
| Act 1: Origins | 10 | Introduction | Fire, Water, Earth |
| Act 2: Conflict | 12 | Rising tension | Wind, Light |
| Act 3: Darkness | 12 | The dark threat | Dark, combinations |
| Act 4: Convergence | 15 | Final battle | All archetypes |
| Epilogue | 5 | Aftermath | Divine |

### Chapter Structure

Each chapter includes:
1. **Story Cutscene** - Narrative exposition
2. **Battle(s)** - 1-3 fights against AI
3. **Rewards** - Cards, gold, cosmetics
4. **Lore Entry** - Unlocked lore content

## Difficulty Modes

### Normal Mode

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| AI Intelligence | Basic |
| Enemy Deck Power | Standard |
| Retry Limit | Unlimited |
| Rewards | Full |

### Hard Mode

Unlocked after completing chapter on Normal.

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| AI Intelligence | Advanced |
| Enemy Deck Power | Optimized |
| Retry Limit | 3 per day |
| Rewards | 2x + bonus |

### Legendary Mode

Unlocked after completing all chapters on Hard.

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| AI Intelligence | Competitive |
| Enemy Deck Power | Meta-level |
| Retry Limit | 1 per week |
| Rewards | 3x + exclusive |

## Story Battles

### Opponent Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Standard** | Normal AI opponent |
| **Boss** | Enhanced AI with unique deck |
| **Puzzle** | Must win in specific way |
| **Survival** | Multiple opponents in sequence |

### Boss Battles

Boss encounters feature:
- Unique character with story role
- Special deck not available to players
- Boss-only cards with unique effects
- Multiple phases in late-game bosses

### Puzzle Battles

Puzzle mode challenges:
- Start with specific board state
- Must achieve objective (usually lethal)
- Tests game knowledge
- Hints available after failures

## Rewards

### Chapter Rewards

| Completion | Reward Type |
|------------|-------------|
| First clear | Gold + cards |
| 3-star clear | Bonus pack |
| Hard mode | 2x rewards |
| Legendary | Exclusive cosmetic |

### Star Rating

Each battle is rated 1-3 stars:

| Stars | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 Star | Complete the battle |
| 2 Stars | Win with 50%+ LP remaining |
| 3 Stars | Win without taking damage |

### Cumulative Rewards

| Total Stars | Reward |
|-------------|--------|
| 50 | Gold x500 |
| 100 | Premium Pack |
| 150 | Exclusive Avatar |
| 200 | Story Card Back |
| All Stars | "Completionist" Title |

## Story Decks

### Loaner Decks

Story mode provides **loaner decks** for new players:
- Pre-built decks matching chapter theme
- Always available regardless of collection
- Cannot be used in PvP

### Using Your Decks

After completing Act 1, you can use your own decks:
- Any deck meeting standard rules
- Replay chapters for better scores
- Harder with personal decks = more rewards

---

## Side Quests

Additional content unlocked during story:

### Character Quests

Focus on specific characters:
- 5 battles per character
- Unlock character backstory
- Earn character-themed rewards

### Challenge Quests

Special rule battles:
- Modified game rules
- Unique objectives
- High-difficulty rewards

### Limited-Time Events

Seasonal story content:
- Holiday-themed chapters
- Exclusive seasonal rewards
- Available for limited time

---

## Lore Collection

### Lore Entries

Complete story content to unlock:
- Character biographies
- World history
- Archetype origins
- Card flavor text

### Lore Gallery

Access from: **Menu > Collection > Lore**
- Browse all unlocked lore
- Listen to voice-acted entries
- View concept art

---

## Tips for Story Mode

1. **Complete Act 1 first** - Teaches game fundamentals
2. **Use loaner decks initially** - Learn archetype synergies
3. **Replay for stars** - 3-star clears give best rewards
4. **Try Hard mode** - 2x rewards worth the challenge
5. **Read the lore** - Understanding world enhances experience

---

## Story Progress

Your story progress includes:
- Chapter completion status
- Star ratings per battle
- Difficulty mode clears
- Lore collection percentage

Sync across devices via account login.
