Every monster card in LTCG belongs to one of 7 elemental attributes, each with unique strengths and thematic identity.

## Attribute Overview

| Attribute | Color | Theme | Playstyle |
|-----------|-------|-------|-----------|
| Fire | Red | Aggression, destruction | Fast, burn damage |
| Water | Blue | Adaptation, control | Flexible, hand advantage |
| Earth | Brown/Green | Defense, stability | Tanks, protection |
| Wind | Cyan | Speed, evasion | Combo, mobility |
| Light | Yellow/White | Healing, protection | Support, recovery |
| Dark | Purple/Black | Power, sacrifice | Risk/reward, disruption |
| Divine | Gold | Transcendence | Unique mechanics |

---

## Fire

**Theme:** Destruction, aggression, burning passion

### Characteristics

- High attack stats
- Burn effects (damage over time)
- Self-destruction synergies
- Board wipes

### Common Keywords

- **Blaze**: Deal damage when summoned
- **Ignite**: Apply burn status to targets
- **Combustion**: Destroy self for powerful effect

### Strengths

- Fast damage output
- Effective removal
- Strong early game

### Weaknesses

- Resource hungry
- Weak to healing
- Can burn out quickly

---

## Water

**Theme:** Adaptation, fluidity, knowledge

### Characteristics

- Balanced stats
- Card draw effects
- Hand manipulation
- Flexible responses

### Common Keywords

- **Flow**: Change position or zone
- **Tidal**: Effects scale with hand size
- **Submerge**: Become untargetable temporarily

### Strengths

- Card advantage
- Adaptable strategies
- Strong mid-game

### Weaknesses

- Slower start
- Less direct damage
- Relies on card access

---

## Earth

**Theme:** Stability, defense, endurance

### Characteristics

- High defense stats
- Protection effects
- LP healing
- Persistent threats

### Common Keywords

- **Fortify**: Gain defense bonuses
- **Rooted**: Cannot be moved or bounced
- **Quake**: Damage based on defense

### Strengths

- Survives longer
- Protects other cards
- Strong late game

### Weaknesses

- Slow damage
- Vulnerable to direct damage
- Can be bypassed

---

## Wind

**Theme:** Speed, freedom, unpredictability

### Characteristics

- High speed/priority
- Position manipulation
- Multi-attack potential
- Evasion abilities

### Common Keywords

- **Swift**: Attack multiple times
- **Gust**: Move cards between zones
- **Cyclone**: Bounce cards to hand

### Strengths

- Fast combos
- Hard to pin down
- Disrupts positioning

### Weaknesses

- Fragile stats
- Weak to defensive walls
- Inconsistent damage

---

## Light

**Theme:** Hope, protection, purification

### Characteristics

- Support abilities
- LP recovery
- Status removal
- Team buffs

### Common Keywords

- **Radiance**: Heal LP when effect resolves
- **Purify**: Remove negative effects
- **Blessing**: Buff allied monsters

### Strengths

- Sustain and recovery
- Enhances other cards
- Removes threats cleanly

### Weaknesses

- Lower attack power
- Dependent on other cards
- Slow to close games

---

## Dark

**Theme:** Power through sacrifice, forbidden knowledge

### Characteristics

- High risk/reward effects
- LP costs for power
- Graveyard manipulation
- Opponent disruption

### Common Keywords

- **Sacrifice**: Pay LP or cards for effect
- **Corrupt**: Weaken or take control
- **Shadow**: Effects from graveyard

### Strengths

- Powerful individual cards
- Disrupts opponent plans
- Graveyard synergy

### Weaknesses

- Self-damaging
- Can backfire
- Risky plays

---

## Divine

**Theme:** Transcendence, unique power

### Characteristics

- Unique effects not found elsewhere
- Often evolution-only
- Highest stat ceilings
- Special summoning requirements

### Common Keywords

- **Divine**: Cannot be negated
- **Transcend**: Ignore standard rules
- **Apex**: Available only at max evolution

### Strengths

- Unmatched power level
- Unique win conditions
- Game-ending effects

### Weaknesses

- Hard to summon
- Limited support cards
- Rare in collections

## Attribute Interactions

### Type Advantages

LTCG does not have hard type advantages, but some effects reference attributes:

| Effect Type | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| Attribute hate | "Destroy target Fire monster" |
| Attribute support | "Fire monsters gain +200 ATK" |
| Attribute conditions | "If you control a Water monster..." |

### Multi-Attribute Cards

Some cards have multiple attributes:
- Count as all listed attributes
- Benefit from all attribute support
- Vulnerable to all attribute hate

---

## Choosing Your Attribute

When building a deck, consider:

1. **Playstyle match** - Pick attributes matching how you like to play
2. **Archetype focus** - Most archetypes favor 1-2 attributes
3. **Meta considerations** - Check what's strong in current ranked
4. **Synergy potential** - Some attribute pairs work well together

### Popular Combinations

| Combo | Synergy |
|-------|---------|
| Fire + Dark | Aggressive self-damage themes |
| Water + Wind | Control with bounce effects |
| Earth + Light | Defensive sustain |
| Light + Dark | Balanced risk/reward |
