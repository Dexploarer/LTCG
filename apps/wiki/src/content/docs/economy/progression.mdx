Level up your account to unlock features, earn rewards, and showcase your experience.

## Player Level

Your **Player Level** represents your overall progression in LTCG.

### Earning XP

| Activity | XP Earned |
|----------|-----------|
| Win a match | 100 |
| Lose a match | 30 |
| Complete a quest | 50-150 |
| Play 3 matches | 50 bonus |
| First win of day | 100 bonus |

### Level Milestones

| Level | Unlock |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Tutorial complete, basic cards |
| 5 | Ranked mode access |
| 10 | Custom deck building |
| 15 | Tournament entry |
| 20 | All archetypes unlocked for viewing |
| 25 | Custom card sleeves |
| 30 | Profile customization |
| 50 | Veteran badge |
| 100 | Master badge |

## Seasonal Progression

### Season Pass

Each ranked season includes a **Season Pass** with two tracks:

#### Free Track
- Unlocked for all players
- Includes: Gold, packs, cosmetics
- 50 levels of rewards

#### Premium Track
- Costs 1000 Gems
- Includes: All free rewards + bonus rewards
- Exclusive card back and avatar
- 20% XP boost

### Season Pass XP

| Activity | Season XP |
|----------|-----------|
| Ranked win | 100 |
| Ranked loss | 40 |
| Daily quest | 200 |
| Weekly quest | 500 |
| Special event | Varies |

## Mastery System

Each **archetype** has its own mastery track.

### Archetype Mastery Levels

| Level | Requirement | Reward |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Play 10 games | Basic card back |
| 2 | Win 25 games | Archetype avatar |
| 3 | Win 100 games | Golden card back |
| 4 | Win 250 games | Prismatic hero card |
| 5 | Win 500 games | Mastery badge |

### Tracking Mastery

Games count toward mastery when:
- You have 15+ cards from that archetype in your deck
- The game lasts at least 5 turns
- PvP matches only (practice doesn't count)

## Daily & Weekly Quests

### Daily Quests

Refresh every 24 hours. You can hold up to 3.

| Quest Type | Example | Reward |
|------------|---------|--------|
| Play games | Play 3 matches | 50 Gold |
| Win games | Win 2 matches | 75 Gold |
| Type-specific | Deal 1000 damage with monsters | 100 Gold |
| Archetype | Win with a Fire deck | 100 Gold |

### Weekly Quests

Refresh every Monday. Larger rewards.

| Quest Type | Example | Reward |
|------------|---------|--------|
| Win streak | Win 5 games in a row | 200 Gold |
| Ranked | Reach a new rank | 300 Gold |
| Collection | Craft 5 cards | 150 Gold |

## Achievement System

One-time achievements that reward various currencies.

### Achievement Categories

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Collection** | Own 100 unique cards, complete an archetype |
| **Victories** | Win 10/50/100/500 games |
| **Ranked** | Reach Silver/Gold/Diamond/Master |
| **Social** | Add 5 friends, gift 10 times |
| **Mastery** | Max mastery on 1/3/5/10 archetypes |

### Achievement Rewards

| Tier | Typical Reward |
|------|----------------|
| Bronze | 50 Gold |
| Silver | 100 Gold + 10 Gems |
| Gold | 200 Gold + 25 Gems |
| Platinum | 500 Gold + 50 Gems + exclusive cosmetic |

## New Player Experience

### Starter Path

New players follow a guided progression:

1. **Tutorial** (Level 1) - Learn game basics
2. **Starter Deck** (Level 2) - Choose your first archetype
3. **Solo Battles** (Level 3) - Practice against AI
4. **First PvP** (Level 4) - Play casual matches
5. **Deck Building** (Level 10) - Create custom decks
6. **Ranked Ready** (Level 5) - Enter competitive play

### Starter Rewards

| Milestone | Reward |
|-----------|--------|
| Complete tutorial | 500 Gold + 10 packs |
| First PvP win | 200 Gold |
| Level 10 | 500 Gold + 5 packs |
| First ranked win | Premium pack |

## Progression Tips

1. **Complete dailies** - Consistent daily play maximizes rewards
2. **Focus one archetype** - Build mastery faster
3. **Buy Season Pass early** - XP boost applies retroactively
4. **Check achievements** - Easy ones often go unclaimed
