LTCG features three main currencies that power your collection growth and deck building.

## Gold

**Gold** is the primary free-to-play currency earned through gameplay.

### Earning Gold

| Activity | Gold Earned |
|----------|-------------|
| Winning a match | 50-100 |
| Daily login | 100 |
| Quest completion | 50-200 |
| First win of the day | 150 bonus |
| Ranked season rewards | 500-2000 |

### Spending Gold

- **Standard Packs**: 100 Gold each
- **Arena Entry**: 150 Gold
- **Cosmetic items**: 200-1000 Gold

---

## Premium Currency (Gems)

**Gems** are the premium currency available for purchase or as rare rewards.

### Obtaining Gems

| Source | Gems |
|--------|------|
| Purchase (small) | 100 for $0.99 |
| Purchase (medium) | 500 for $4.99 |
| Purchase (large) | 1100 for $9.99 |
| Season Pass rewards | 50-200 |
| Special events | 10-50 |

### Gem Purchases

- **Premium Packs**: 150 Gems (guaranteed rare+)
- **Season Pass**: 1000 Gems
- **Legendary Pack**: 500 Gems (guaranteed legendary)
- **Cosmetic bundles**: 200-1500 Gems

---

## Dust

**Dust** is the crafting currency obtained by disenchanting unwanted cards.

### Dust from Disenchanting

| Rarity | Dust Received |
|--------|---------------|
| Common | 5 |
| Uncommon | 20 |
| Rare | 100 |
| Epic | 400 |
| Legendary | 1600 |

### Dust for Crafting

| Rarity | Dust Required |
|--------|---------------|
| Common | 40 |
| Uncommon | 100 |
| Rare | 400 |
| Epic | 1600 |
| Legendary | 3200 |

:::tip[Crafting Ratio]
Disenchanting gives roughly 25% of the crafting cost, so craft wisely!
:::

---

## Currency Tips

1. **Save Gold for events** - Special events often offer better value packs
2. **Don't disenchant early** - Wait until you understand the meta before dusting cards
3. **Complete daily quests** - Consistent daily play maximizes Gold income
4. **Buy the Season Pass** - If you play regularly, it offers the best gem value
